## Business context

In oil and gas, investment decisions must demonstrate that the project can clear the hurdle rate. The hurdle rate --- often set around 10--15% --- represents the minimum return on capital employed (ROCE) that companies require before committing billions of dollars to new exploration, production, or refining projects.

But as the chart above shows, the actual returns achieved by the industry's largest players have varied widely over time.
